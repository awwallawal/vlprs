Vehicle Loan Processing & Receivables System

A Digital System of Record for the Oyo State Government Vehicle Loan Scheme

The Situation

The Vehicle Loan Scheme serves 3,100+ civil servants across 63 MDAs, with total obligations exceeding ₦2.6 billion. Today, every aspect of this portfolio is administered manually — 63 separate spreadsheets, independent calculations, paper-based records, and reports compiled over days.

This manual approach produces three critical risks: workers are over-deducted beyond loan completion with no automated safeguard; calculation errors compound undetected across 63 independent sources; and the Accountant General has no real-time visibility into the fund's position, outstanding obligations, or MDA compliance — with scheme status queries requiring 2–3 days of manual compilation.

The Solution

VLPRS is a purpose-built, secure web application that replaces fragmented manual processes with a single, authoritative digital system of record. It automates all financial calculations, provides real-time executive visibility, and introduces the Auto-Stop Certificate — guaranteeing deductions halt the moment a loan reaches zero balance.

What VLPRS Delivers

  • Executive Dashboard — 4 headline numbers (Active Loans, Total Exposure, Fund Available, Monthly Recovery) accessible from any device in under 3 seconds
  • Automated Computation — All loan calculations handled by a single, verified engine covering all 4 tiers — no manual math
  • Auto-Stop Certificate — Automatically generated at loan completion, officially halting deductions and protecting workers
  • Simplified MDA Submissions — 8 fields instead of 17 columns, with instant confirmation
  • Immutable Audit Trail — Every financial transaction is permanent, traceable, and audit-ready
  • PDF Reports in Seconds — Executive summaries, MDA compliance, and variance reports — all branded with the Oyo State crest
  • Retirement Pipeline — Proactive forecasting of retiring staff with outstanding obligations and gratuity receivable tracking
  • Automated Alerts — Email notifications for overdue submissions, inactive loans, and approaching retirements
