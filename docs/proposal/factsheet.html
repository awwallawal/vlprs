Six Critical Challenges
⚠

Over-Deductions

Workers continue to be deducted months after loans are fully repaid. No automated mechanism to stop deductions at zero balance. The #1 source of grievances.

⚙

Calculation Errors

63 MDAs applying the same formula independently produce divergent results. ~30% discrepancy rate in sampled records. Errors compound silently.

👁

Zero Visibility

No real-time view of total obligations, available fund, MDA compliance, or loan status. The fund's position is unknown at any given moment.

⏱

3-Day Reporting

Consolidated scheme reports require manual aggregation from 63 sources — taking 2–3 working days per cycle. Urgent queries cannot be answered promptly.

🔎

No Audit Trail

Spreadsheet records provide no systematic trail of changes. When discrepancies are found, their origin cannot be traced. Exposure under audit review.

📅

Retirement Gaps

No forecasting of staff retiring with outstanding balances. Gratuity receivables discovered reactively. Recovery opportunities are missed.

These are not failures of effort — they are structural limitations of managing a ₦2.6B+ revolving fund with spreadsheets and paper files.
